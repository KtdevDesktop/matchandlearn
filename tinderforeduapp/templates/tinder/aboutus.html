About our App

Our app is about socialization (And fate. Maybe...)

Maybe you'll find someone that you want to improve your grade with.

MATCH AND LEARN is matching website that let you find someone. But not an orinary ways. You need to find someone by subject! So this website is suit for someone who looking for someone to teach you and you can teach them back. It's a WIN-WIN! So your grade is improve and your socialization is improve. More grade! More friend!

  1. ADD YOUR EXPERTISE SUBJECT - Everyone has a subject that you're good with. Add them in our site to let everyone know that you can teach them in that subject.
  2. SEARCH FOR YOUR POOR SUBJECT - Again. Everyone has a subject that you're not good. You can find someone that can teach you. So let's search.
  3. MATCH AND LEARN - When you found someone. Ask them to match you. If you're matched, Good! Now you have someone to teach you in that subject.
